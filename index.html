About me

My background story...

I'm Sellie, an Indonesian girl who loves travelling around the world. What I like most about it, is meeting new people, discovering new things and coming back home with a lot of stories to tell. Travelling gives me a better view of many things. My life is full with things to "learn from yesterday, live for today and hope for tomorrow"

What I do now...

I am studying at Restart Network now. I follow "ONE program" and I hope it can help me to reach my dreams. Travelling around the world is one of them :)

I am also a member of Toastmasters International organization. I used to go to Toastmasters in Jakarta, but now I am a member of Rotterdam Toastmasters Club. The best thing about being a member is I can join any club meeting around the world!

What I wish for the future

TRAVEL MORE!

What will you find in my website?

Welcome to my page. In this blog I share my travelling stories and experiences, to inspire you for one of your next trips or - hopefully - make you want to travel as well. I want to show you there's so much more to traveling than just seeing things. It's about discovering, feeling and living the experiences. About getting out of your comfort zone and diving head first into the great adventure that is life. About realizing that we need to discover this world and learn from it. Enjoy!
